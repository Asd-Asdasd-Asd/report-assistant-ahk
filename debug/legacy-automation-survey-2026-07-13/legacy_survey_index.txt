MedEx Legacy Automation Survey
Date: 2026-07-13
Purpose:
Inventory the unmerged legacy AHK functions and decide whether each function
should be retained directly, migrated to UIA, replaced by relative positioning,
or kept as coordinate/image-based automation.
Classification:
1. Direct retain
   Pure keyboard or text behavior with no UI dependency.
2. UIA migration candidate
   A visible control or menu item may support Invoke, Select, Toggle, or Value.
3. Relative-position fallback
   Container or anchor elements are visible, but the target control itself is not.
4. Coordinate/image fallback
   No reliable accessibility element is exposed.
